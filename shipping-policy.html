Shipping Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Tenon Hardware delivers across Greater Male' and to islands throughout the Maldives. Here's what to expect when your order ships.

Delivery Areas

  • Male', Hulhumale' & Vilimale': Same-day or next-day delivery for orders placed before 2pm
  • Other islands: Delivered via inter-island ferry or cargo, typically within 3–7 business days depending on transport schedules

Delivery Fees

Delivery charges are calculated at checkout based on your location and the size/weight of your order. Free delivery is available on qualifying orders within Greater Male' — look for the "Free delivery on this order" message in your cart.

Order Processing

Orders are processed and dispatched from our warehouse once payment is confirmed. You'll receive a confirmation message with your order details, and we'll notify you when your order is on its way.

Large or Bulky Items

Some building materials and bulk hardware orders may require special transport arrangements. Our team will contact you directly to coordinate delivery timing for these items.

Tracking Your Order

You can check the status of your order from the "Track Order" section of your account, or by contacting our support team with your order number.

Questions?

For any delivery questions, contact us at sales@tenon.mv or +960 7997970.
