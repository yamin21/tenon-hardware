Professional-grade impact performance

The DeWalt DCF887 Impact Driver is engineered for the demands of professional tradespeople. The brushless motor delivers maximum efficiency — extending motor life and providing up to 57% more runtime compared to brushed alternatives, so you keep working longer between charges.

Three-speed settings with Precision Drive in Speed 1 give you complete control for delicate fastening tasks. Whether you're driving long deck screws into hardwood or assembling furniture with fine fixings, the DCF887 adapts to the task with precision.

What's in the box

  • DCF887 18V XR Brushless Impact Driver (body only)
  • Belt hook (left or right mountable)
  • 1/4″ hex chuck insert bit holder
  • Kit bag
